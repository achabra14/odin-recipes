Lasagna

This classic Italian dish can be made easily. Both vegetarian and meat options are available!

Ingedients

  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Beef
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Put pasta in plate
  2. Put cheese and beef on pasta
  3. Mix the sauce into the pasta
  4. Cook in oven on 425 for 30 minutes
  5. Sprinkle with basil
  6. Let cool for 15 minutes and enjoy!
