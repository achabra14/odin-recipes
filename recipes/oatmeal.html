Oatmeal

Quick and easy breakfast option that will give you energy for the day.

Ingedients

  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1 cup milk or water
  • 1 TBSP Peanut Butter
  • 1/4 cup almonds

Steps

  1. Mix oats and milk or water
  2. Microwave for 2 minutes
  3. Let stand for 2 minutes
  4. Microwave for 1 minute
  5. Let stand for 2 minutes
  6. Add Peanut Butter and Almonds
  7. Give the remains of peanut butter spoon to dog!
